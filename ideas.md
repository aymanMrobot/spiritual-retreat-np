<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Desert Minimalism / Wabi-Sabi**
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Imperfect Beauty**: Embracing the natural textures of the riad (tadelakt, clay, wood) and the organic flow of yoga.
  2. **Serene Spaciousness**: Prioritizing breath and stillness through generous negative space.
  3. **Grounded Earthiness**: Connecting the digital experience to the physical location (Atlas mountains, soil, sun).
  4. **Quiet Luxury**: Elegant, understated typography and imagery that speaks of exclusivity without shouting.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  *   **Palette**: Terracotta, warm sand, deep olive green, faded indigo, and soft cream.
  *   **Intent**: To evoke the warmth of the Moroccan sun and the coolness of the riad's shadows. These colors ground the user and invite a sense of calm and restoration.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  *   **Asymmetric Balance**: Moving away from rigid grids to a more fluid, organic arrangement of elements, mimicking the flow of a vinyasa practice.
  *   **Overlapping Layers**: Images and text slightly overlapping to create depth and a sense of immersion, like layers of history in the riad.
  *   **Vertical Flow**: A strong vertical rhythm that guides the eye gently down the page, like a deep exhale.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1.  **Organic Shapes**: Subtle, hand-drawn lines or soft, amorphous shapes as background elements to break up straight lines.
  2.  **Texture Overlays**: Faint grain or paper textures to give a tactile feel, reminiscent of ancient manuscripts or the earth itself.
  3.  **Floating Imagery**: Images that appear to float or drift slightly on scroll, enhancing the feeling of weightlessness.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  *   **Slow & Deliberate**: Transitions should be slow and smooth (ease-in-out), discouraging rushing.
  *   **Hover Breath**: Elements gently expand or brighten on hover, simulating an inhale.

  **Animation**:
  *   **Parallax Scroll**: Gentle parallax effects on background images to create depth.
  *   **Fade & Rise**: Content fades in and rises slowly as it enters the viewport.

  **Typography System**:
  *   **Headings**: **Cormorant Garamond** (Serif) - Elegant, high-contrast, and timeless.
  *   **Body**: **Montserrat** (Sans-serif) - Clean, geometric, and highly readable, providing a modern counterpoint to the serif headings.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Maghreb Modernism / Neo-Moorish**
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Geometric Harmony**: Drawing inspiration from zellige tile patterns and Islamic geometry, but interpreting them in a clean, modern way.
  2. **Vibrant Vitality**: Celebrating the energy of life and movement through bold (but controlled) color accents.
  3. **Structured Elegance**: A more grid-based approach that reflects the architectural precision of the riad.
  4. **Cultural Fusion**: Blending traditional Moroccan motifs with contemporary web design trends.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  *   **Palette**: Majorelle Blue, Emerald Green, Gold, and White.
  *   **Intent**: To reflect the vibrant colors of Marrakech and the lushness of the gardens. These colors energize and inspire.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  *   **Modular Grid**: A clear, flexible grid system that allows for organized content presentation, echoing the tiled courtyards.
  *   **Split Screens**: Using split-screen layouts to juxtapose imagery with text, creating a dialogue between the visual and the verbal.
  *   **Framed Content**: Content sections framed by subtle borders or geometric patterns.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1.  **Geometric Patterns**: Subtle SVG patterns based on zellige designs used as backgrounds or dividers.
  2.  **Arched Frames**: Images cropped into arch shapes (reminiscent of the riad's architecture).
  3.  **Gold Accents**: Fine lines or buttons in a metallic gold finish to add a touch of luxury.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  *   **Crisp & Responsive**: Interactions should feel snappy and precise.
  *   **Pattern Reveals**: Hovering over sections might reveal a subtle geometric pattern overlay.

  **Animation**:
  *   **Slide & Reveal**: Content slides in from the sides or is revealed from behind masks.
  *   **Staggered Entry**: List items or grid elements appear in a staggered sequence.

  **Typography System**:
  *   **Headings**: **Playfair Display** (Serif) - Classic, high-contrast, with a touch of drama.
  *   **Body**: **Lato** (Sans-serif) - Friendly, humanist sans-serif that is easy to read.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Ethereal Wellness / Atmospheric**
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Light & Air**: The design should feel weightless, filled with light and airiness.
  2. **Soft Focus**: Using blur and soft gradients to create a dreamlike atmosphere.
  3. **Emotional Connection**: Focusing on the feeling of the retreat – peace, connection, transformation.
  4. **Fluidity**: Everything should flow like water, with no hard edges or abrupt stops.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  *   **Palette**: Soft Sage, Pale Blush, Misty Blue, and Warm Grey.
  *   **Intent**: To create a soothing, spa-like environment that immediately relaxes the user.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  *   **Full-Screen Immersion**: Large, full-screen background images or videos that immerse the user in the setting.
  *   **Centered Focus**: Key messages and calls to action centered on the screen for clarity and focus.
  *   **Minimal UI**: Hiding non-essential UI elements to reduce cognitive load.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1.  **Gradient Overlays**: Soft, multi-color gradients that shift slowly.
  2.  **Glassmorphism**: Frosted glass effects for cards and navigation menus.
  3.  **Micro-interactions**: Subtle ripples or glows on interaction.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  *   **Fluid & Continuous**: Scrolling should feel like floating.
  *   **Soft Feedback**: Buttons and links have soft, glowing states rather than hard color changes.

  **Animation**:
  *   **Slow Zoom**: Background images slowly zoom in or out.
  *   **Blur In**: Text blurs into focus.

  **Typography System**:
  *   **Headings**: **Tenor Sans** (Sans-serif) - Humanist, open, and friendly with a unique character.
  *   **Body**: **Nunito** (Sans-serif) - Rounded and soft, reinforcing the gentle vibe.
</idea>
